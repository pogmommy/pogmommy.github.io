Congratulations for graduating from Penelope Gwen University!

Why does my degree appear blank?

Due to the high volume of traffic from gradautes attempting to access their degree, the PenU Student Portal might display a blank degree. If you experience this issue, please try downloading your degree again, or try in another browser.

Is Penelope Gwen University an accredited university?

PenU is just as accredited a university as Prager University. However unlike Prager University, PenU is not a source of misinformation.

How much is tuition to PenU?

$0.00 USD per semester, with a 10% rebate available if enrolled for a full year. Further contributions can be made here.

Where is the PenU campus located?

Penelope Gwen University is located at 2 15th St NW, Washington, DC 20024. Feel free to visit and ask for a tour!

What should I do if I have COVID-19 symptoms?

Wear a mask, seek medical care, and avoid any public meetings, including but not limited to on-campus courses at Penelope Gwen University.

What are the steps to acceptance into PenU?

Take a shower, have a snack, and press the button above.

Does PenU accept international students?

No, in the sense that Penelope Gwen University does not recognize state or nation borders. Anyone is welcome in Penelope Gwen University without regard to geographical barriers as percieved by any other institution.

Are PenU degree certificates subject to revokation?

Yes. Penelope Gwen University graduation certifications are automatically null and void if the recipient expresses views contrary to those outlined in the Penelope Gwen University code of ethics.
